# The Munich Event - Working Agreement

This working group is built on collaboration, respect, and curiosity. We are here to improve our way of working together.

## Principles

* Be respectful and assume good intent.
* Discuss ideas, not people.
* Ask questions before making assumptions.
* Keep discussions constructive and focused on improving the work.

## Working with the Repository

* Do not delete or significantly rewrite someone else's work without discussing it first.
* Open an issue or pull request for larger changes.
* Explain the reasoning behind non-obvious decisions.
* Prefer small, incremental improvements over large, disruptive changes.
* If you disagree with a proposal, explain why and suggest an alternative.

## Collaboration

* Everyone is encouraged to contribute, regardless of experience.
* Feedback should be specific, respectful, and actionable.
* Accept that not every proposal will be adopted.
* We optimize for learning and long-term quality, not for being right.

## Final Rule

When in doubt, communicate first. A short discussion is usually cheaper than cleaning up misunderstandings later.
